Minor Event
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Minor Events are short-lasting events in The Pit. Ranging from instantaneous to several minutes long, they come in many different forms. Minor events typically occur every eight minutes, but they can appear as early as four minutes to as late as twenty-two. They have the potential to reward users with gold, XP, and in the case of the Auction event, Renown and miscellaneous items. Currently, nine exist.
  • Dragon Egg
  • Giant Cake
  • King of the Ladder
  • Auction
  • King of the Hill
  • Quick Maths
  • Care Package
  • 2x Rewards
  • Everyone Gets a Bounty
