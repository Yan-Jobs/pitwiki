Apostle to RNGesus
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Apostle to RNGesus is a killstreak in the Hypixel Pit.

When activated (every 25 kills), a number from 1 to 100 will be rolled, and based on the number, some of the following effects may occur.

  • If the number was a multiple of 2: Gain Regneration I (0:20).
  • If the number was a multiple of 3: Gain Speed I (0:15).
  • If the number was a multiple of 4: Gain Regeneration II (0:10).
  • If the number was a multiple of 7: Gain between 500-5000 gold.
  • If the number was a multiple of 8: Gain between 500-5000 XP.
  • If the number was a multiple of 12: Gain 4&hearts-; absorption.
  • If the number was a multiple of 13: Deal +25% damage for 30 seconds.
  • If the number was 27 or 43: Gain 1 renown.
  • If the number was 13 or 66: Gain 3 Chunks of Vile.
  • If the number was 77 or 88: Gain a Mystic Sword, Mystic Bow, or a standard color of Fresh Pants.
  • If the number was 42: Gain +10&hearts-; absorption.
  • If the number was 50: Gain an RNGesus Helmet (diamond helmet).
  • If the number was 99: Get struck by lightning for 4&hearts-; true damage.

Trivia


The most effects you can receive at once is 4, with a roll of 56.
