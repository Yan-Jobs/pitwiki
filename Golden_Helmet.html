Golden Helmet
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
The Golden Helmet is a mystic item given as a reward by the King from completing the King's Quest on the Castle map. Unlike most mystic items, it cannot be placed in the Mystic Well and by extension cannot be repaired using Magnum Opus or enchanted in the Mystic Well. The helmet has 50 lives.

Its ability to increase one's XP output and defense makes it a popular armor piece for many players. Trivia
Before The Pit 1.0.5, the golden helmet had 5 lives. Due to an change in the update that made it much more difficult to retain mystic lives, the helmet's lives were increased.
