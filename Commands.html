Commands
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Commands are ways players can modify aspects of their game. This article lists the commands that are exclusive to the Pit.

CommandDescription
/oofInstantly die. All effects that would occur on a normal death still occur. Shares a 10-second cooldown with /spawn.
/spawnInstantly teleport to spawn. Shares a 10-second cooldown with /oof. Cannot be used in combat or while on a megastreak.
/trade [username]Sends a trade request to the player inputted. Unlocks at level 60 of each prestige.
/offer [username] [gold]Sends a trade offer of the item you are holding to the inputted username for a specific amount of gold. The recipient has 30 seconds to accept the offer. Offers count towards trade limits. Unlocks at level 60 of each prestige.
/pitchatOpens a GUI to toggle certain messages in the game's chat. From left-to-right, the player has the option to toggle chat messages for bounties, killstreaks, prestiges, minor events, their kill feed, player chat, and miscellaneous (such as tips, notes, gold pickups, and participation XP). Unlocks at level 5.
/view [username]Opens a GUI displaying stats on the selected player. Stats include the player's level, gold, hours played, renown, armor, inventory, ender chest, selected killstreaks, and perks.

Trivia

The /view command's alias, /blue, was translated into a variety of languages. Commands that mimic /view include /azul (Spanish), /蓝 (Chinese), and /μπλε (Greek).
