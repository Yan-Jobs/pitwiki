Quests
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Quests are built-in missions in the Hypixel server. In most games, such as The Pit, most quests reset once every day, with a subset resetting once per week. Quests and contracts can be activated at the Quest Master NPC at spawn. The Pit grants gold and Hypixel (not Pit) Experience. Daily quests reset every day at midnight Eastern Time and the weekly quest resets on Friday at midnight Eastern Time.
There are three quests in The Pit:
  • Daily Quest: Hunter: Kill 25 players. Grants 1,000 Pit Gold and 2,000 Hypixel Experience.
  • Daily Quest: Contracted: Complete a contract. Grants 1,000 Pit Gold and 3,000 Hypixel Experience.
  • Weekly Quest: Double Up: Collect 10,000 gold. Grants 10,000 Pit Pit Gold and 7,000 Hypixel Experience.
