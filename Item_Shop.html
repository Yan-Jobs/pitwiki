Item Shop
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

The Item Shop is an NPC in the spawn area of the pit. Various upgrades and helpful items can be purchased from the item shop, however, most of them are lost on death.

By default, the following items can be purchased from the Renown Shop:

NameGold CostDescription
Diamond Sword150gA diamond sword. Deals +20% damage to players who have a bounty.
Obsidian40gA stack of 8 obsidian.
Diamond Chestplate500gA diamond chestplate.
Diamond Boots300gA pair of diamond boots.
Golden Pickaxe500gA golden pickaxe. When clicking an obsidian block twice with it, destroy it and up to four connected obsidian blocks above it.

In addition to the default items, nine items can be added to the Item Shop by unlocking them in the Renown Shop.
NamePrestige RequiredRenown CostGold CostDescription
Iron PackPrestige I5 renown100gAn iron chestplate, leggings, and boots.
Obsidian StackPrestige II5 renown240gA stack of 64 obsidian. 25% cheaper than the original.
Combat SpadePrestige III10 renown750gA diamond shovel modified to deal the same damage as a diamond sword. Deals +0.5♥ damage per diamond armor piece the target is wearing.
Diamond LeggingsPrestige III20 renown1200gA pair of diamond leggings.
First-Aid EggPrestige V15 renown200gA mooshroom egg. When right-clicked, heal 2.5♥ but lose the Speed effect if you have it. 30 second cooldown, minus 5 for every player killed during the cooldown. While on cooldown, appears as a skeleton egg.
Bounty SolventPrestige VI10 renown575gA potion. When drank, grants the Bounty Solvent effect (1:00). Effect time remaining can be viewed in the scoreboard. While you have the effect, take 30% less damage from players with a 1000g bounty, and earn +50% gold from claimed bounties.
Jump Boost IVPrestige VII20 renown150gA potion. When drank, grants Jump Boost IV (0:30).
Tactical InsertionPrestige VIII20 renown100gA blaze rod. When right-clicked, the spawn point for your next death only is set to your current location. 60 second cooldown.
Pants BundlePrestige XII20 renown50gA minecart. When right-clicked, stores the first 10 Fresh Pants in your inventory inside it.
