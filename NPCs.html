NPCs
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

NPCs are characters scattered around the Pit that serve assorted purposes.

Most NPCs offer trades and upgrades, but some have more miscellaneous purposes.

The list of NPCs is as follows:

LocationNameDescription
SpawnItems (Non-Permanent)Allows the player to access the Item Shop.
Upgrades (Permanent)Allows the player to access the Upgrades Shop.
Quest MasterAllows the player to access Quests and Contracts.
StatsAllows the player to view their Statistics.
PrestigeAllows the player to Prestige and access the Renown Shop.
The KeeperAllows the player return to the Hypixel Main Lobby without using commands.
Castle Map exclusiveThe KingAllows the player to start and complete the King's Quest.
The BakerAllows the player to turn in materials to receive a Mini_Cake.
SewerfishAccepts 64 Sewer Rubbish in exchange for Fresh Sewer Pants.
Hay (salesman)Removes all Packaged Bale from your inventory, giving 16 gold per bale removed.
Bread (salesman)Removes all Yummy Bread from your inventory, giving 24 gold per loaf removed.
Fish (salesman)Removes all types of fish from your inventory, giving 1 gold per fish removed.
Genesis Map exclusiveAngelAllows the player to access the Angel faction menu.
DemonAllows the player to access the Demon faction menu.


Spawn NPC menus can also be accessed through the use of the Trash Panda enchantment.
