Fishing
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Fishing is an activity in The Pit. It is the only way to obtain certain items, such as Aqua Pants. Fishing can be done in any body of water, regardless of size.

Fishing can be done in any body of water, regardless of size.

When fishing without the Fishing Club renown upgrade, the following items can be obtained:

  • 1 or 3 gold ingots
  • Diamond swords

Fishing can be significantly upgraded with the Fishing Club renown upgrade, unlocked at prestige III.

  • Kloonfish, granting 2♥ of absorption
  • Salmon, granting Regeneration III (0:03)
  • Diamond chestplate, leggings, and boots (Fishing Club III)
  • Fresh aqua pants (Fishing Club V)

Trivia

In the Genesis map, players can fish in the water columns in the spawn area.

Both salmon and kloonfish have eight different sizes: Nearly microscopic, Tiny, Small, Medium, Large, X-Large, Massive, and Gargantuan. Fish sizes are cosmetic and appear as a prefix before the fish's name. Nearly microscopic and Gargantuan fish are extremely rare.
