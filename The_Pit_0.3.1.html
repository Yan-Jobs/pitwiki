The Pit 0.3.1
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

The Pit 0.3.1 was a very minor version of the Hypixel Pit. It was an intermediary update between The Pit 0.3 and The Pit 0.3.5.

Changes:

  • Fixed a damage bug related to the Kung Fu Knowledge and relogging.
  • Changed the Mineman perk's pickaxe to Efficiency IV.
  • Disabled placing blocks as beast in the Beast event.
