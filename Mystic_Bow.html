Mystic Bows are mystic items obtained extremely rarely from killing players while the killer has the Mysticism renown upgrade.

While unenchanted, Mystic Bows are functionally identical to normal bows. However, they can be upgraded in the Mystic Well. Upgrading to tier I costs 1000g, upgrading to tier II costs 4000g, and upgrading to tier III costs 8000g, requires Mysticism IX, and requires a sacrifice of a randomly chosen color of Mystic Pants.

Enchanting Mystic Bows gives them a random number of lives that increases on every tier and removes their Kept on Death status. Lives decrease by 1 on death, or by 3 if you log out while in combat. Depleting all lives on a mystic item "breaks" the mystic item, destroying it forever.

When enchanted, Mystic Bows gain random enchantments with varying effects. Upon upgrading to tier I, they gain 1-2 'tokens' (total tiers) of a low-grade enchantment. When upgrading to tier II, they gain 1-2 more tokens of any enchantment, and at tier III, they gain 1-4 more tokens, up to 8 total. Enchanted Mystic Bows may alsy have certain prefixes if their properties match certain criteria.

  • Legendary: 8 total tokens
  • Extraordinary: 2 unique rare enchantments
  • Artifact: 100 max lives
  • Bountiful: 3 unique resource enchantments
  • Overpowered: At least 7 tokens and 100 lives
  • Miraculous: 2 unique rare enchantments and 100 lives
  • Unthinkable: 3 unique rare enchantments (Not possible without admin interference)
  • One in a Million: 3 unique rare enchantments and 100 lives (Not possible without admin interference)
