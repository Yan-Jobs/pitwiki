From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Mystic Swords are mystic items obtained extremely rarely from killing players while the killer has the Mysticism renown upgrade.

While unenchanted, Mystic swords act as Sharpness 2 golden swords. However, they can be upgraded in the Mystic Well. Upgrading to tier I costs 1,000g, upgrading to tier II costs 4,000g, and upgrading to tier III costs 8,000g, requires Mysticism IX, and requires a sacrifice of a randomly chosen color of Mystic Pants.

Enchanting Mystic Swords gives them a random number of lives that increases on every tier and removes their Kept on Death status. Lives decrease by 1 on death, or by 3 if their user logs out while in combat. Depleting all lives on a mystic item "breaks" it, destroying it forever.

When enchanted, Mystic Swords gain random enchantments with varying effects. Upon upgrading to tier I, they gain 1–2 "tokens" – total tiers – of a low-grade enchantment. When upgrading to tier II, they gain 1–2 more tokens of any enchantment (at most one of which can be RARE!), and at tier III, they gain 1–4 more tokens (at most three of which can be RARE!) – up to 8 total.

One Mystic Sword enchant, Hidden Jewel, cannot be obtained from normal enchanting. A Tier I sword enchanted with Hidden Jewel can only be obtained from Uberstreaks.

NameDescription
Hidden JewelIf 117 players are killed with this sword without dying, this sword turns into a Tier I Sword with 3 or 5 lives and a random Tier III enchant. Returning to spawn without dying does not reset the kills.

Enchanted Mystic Swords also have certain prefixes if their properties match certain criteria.

  • Legendary: 8 total tokens
  • Extraordinary: 2 unique rare enchantments
  • Artifact: 100 maximum lives
  • Bountiful: 3 unique resource enchantments
  • Overpowered: At least 7 tokens and 100 maximum lives
  • Miraculous: 2 unique rare enchantments and 100 maximum lives
  • Unthinkable: 3 unique rare enchantments (Requires Hidden Jewel)
  • One in a Million: 3 unique rare enchantments and 100 lives (Requires Hidden Jewel)
