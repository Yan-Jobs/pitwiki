Stats, short for "statistics", are numeric values players can view both in-game and online, through the Stats NPC and various websites.

The list of Rage enchants is as follows:

If there is a level III Rage enchant on the pants, they gain the Malding prefix. If the pants have 8 total tokens, they gain the Manic prefix.
