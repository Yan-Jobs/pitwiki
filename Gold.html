Gold
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Gold (g) is an essential resource in The Pit. Similarly to XP, accumulating it is required to prestige. A player can view how much gold they currently have in the sidebar on the right side of their screen.

The following are a variety of ways players may obtain gold:

  • Killing players rewards a base of 10 gold.
  • Killing players with a bounty will award the killer with the bounty. Using a Bounty Solvent will award the player with +50% more gold.
  • Picking up ingots rewards 2 gold. This amount can be increased with the Gold Boost passive, the Trickle-down perk, and the Pebble enchantment on mystic pants.
  • Standing on the structure during the King of the Ladder event gives gold.
  • Completing personal bonus tasks in certain major events rewards gold.
  • Eating cherries in the Giant Cake give 150 gold, increasing by 37.5g for every 50 slices eaten.
  • Being one of the first 5 players to answer a Quick Maths question grants 500 gold.
  • Clicking the dragon egg rewards 25 gold per click.
  • Completing the King's Quest gives 10,000 gold and an additional 2,000 gold for every prestige the player has.
  • Completing contracts give between 400 and 5,000 gold. This amount can be increased by as much as +100% with the Negotiator mystic enchant.
  • Completing daily quests gives 1,000 gold for completing them, and completing the weekly Double Up quest gives 10,000.

  • Killstreaks such as Second Gapple, Arquebusier, Khanate, and Apostle to RNGesus give the user gold.
  • Mystic enchantments, such as Strike Gold and Pitpocket, allow players to earn and steal gold by hitting players.
  • 200 gold can be found as a loot item in Sewer Treasures.

In addition to the base 10 gold on kill, there are multiple ways to increase the base gold earned on kill. The maximum gold earned on kill is 2,500g. The only exception is on Pit Day, when it is increased to 5,000g.

  • The first 3 kills every life grant +4 gold.
  • Killing players with more armor than you gives up to an additional +16 gold. The value is calculated by doing the following:
    • The "values" of the killer and victim's armor pieces are added up.
    • Iron armor gives +2g. Diamond armor gives +4g. All other forms of armor, regardless of their effective defense, award 0g.
    • The killer's armor value is subtracted from the victim's armor value. If the result is less than 0g, it is increased to 0g.
  • The Promotion!! renown upgrade upgrades the Assistant (to the) Streaker perk to give +2 gold every assist. The Spammer perk also gives +2 gold on every assist.
  • The El Gato passive gives +5 gold on kill for up to your first five kills each life.
  • The Gold Bump mystic enchantment gives up to +12 base gold per kill and the Moctezuma enchantment gives up to +18 base gold per kill.
  • The Demon Faction tier VII perk gives +0.2 gold on kill for every time it has been claimed, up to 15 times (+3 gold).

The gold earned is then multiplied by all of the following that are applicable:

  • The Renown Gold Boost upgrade gives up to +25% more gold on kill.
  • The Gold Boost passive increases gold on kills by up to +50%, or +60% with the One-Upper renown upgrade.
    • enchant gives up to +18 base gold.
  • The Gold Stack killstreak increases gold earned on kill by a varying amount, depending on your current prestige.
  • The Co-op Cat perk gives +50% gold on assists.
  • 2x Rewards, King of the Ladder, Team Deathmatch, and Rage Pit increase gold earned from killing players by +100%. The King of the Hill event adds +300%.
  • The Conglomerate perk removes the ability to earn XP, transmuting every XP earned into 0.20g. Conglomerate gives an additional 20% gold buff to all assists, regardless of whether the player has purchased the perk.
  • The Celebrity renown upgrade gives +100% gold from kills and assists.
  • Shooting a player with the Spammer perk equipped gives a bonus of +200% gold on that player.
  • Certain megastreaks also give gold multipliers. Overdrive gives +50% gold, Beastmode gives +75% gold, Highlander gives +110% gold, and Hermit gives +5% gold for every 10 kills, with a maximum of 200 kills (+75% gold).
  • Killing a "noob", or someone level 20 or lower, grants −9% gold.

In order to prestige, a player needs to have grinded a certain amount of gold. This does not necessarily mean that the player needs to have that amount of gold in their inventory; the gold simply needs to have been earned at any point during the prestige. Actions such as trading do not count towards the gold requirement.

Players are able to trade up to 50,000 gold per day. Additionally, gold can also be used to purchase shop items, perks, passives, killstreaks, and items from Auction minor events.

Trivia

Before The Pit 1.0.5, gold requirements were extremely high, requiring as much as 18,480,000g grinded to prestige. The gold requirements for all prestiges are now capped at 2,000,000g each.
