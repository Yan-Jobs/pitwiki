Spire
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Spire is a major event in the Hypixel Pit. Like all major events, when it occurs, 3 minutes advance warning is given. Spire lasts for 5 minutes.
Two minutes before Spire starts, the spire structure generates in a preset quadrant of the pit. One minute later, a tunnel with a portal at the bottom appears under the spawn area, which will be sealed with rainbow glass.
When the event starts, the glass will be removed, and players will be able to enter the portal, taking them to the first floor of the spire. Upon entering the spire, all items in the player's inventory will be sent to their stash, and players may only fight with the provided items from each floor and their perk items.
There are nine floors of the spire. If a player gets a kill in Spire, they will gain souls and move up a floor. If a player dies in Spire, they will lose souls and move down a floor. If the player dies on floor 1 or kills on floor 9, their floor will not change. Placement is determined by how many souls a player has at the end of the event.
During Spire, the following gameplay changes take effect:
  • The Vampire and Barbarian perks are disabled.
  • Healing items are replaced with Fractured Souls (4♥ heal, 3♥ absorption, Resistance II (0:03)). Earning soups on assist is disabled.
  • The Devil Chicks, Explosive and TNT enchantments are disabled.
Spire provides the player with armor and a Spireblade on every floor (except floor 1, where the player only receives a Spireblade). Spireblades are special variants of vanilla swords enchanted with Soulripper.
  • Soulripper: Deal +15% damage per consecutive hit on the same player (stacking up to 75%). Hitting a different player resets the stack.

The following rewards can be obtained during Spire:
  • 2 renown and 2,000 gold if the player scored within the top 3
  • or 2 renown if the player scored within the top 3, as well as 1 additional renown for reaching floor 6 at least once
  • 1,500 XP and 1,500 gold for reaching floor 6 at least once
Trivia
The achievement Glass Ceiling is rewarded for reaching floor 9 of the Spire. It rewards the player with 10 achievement points.
It is possible to enter the Spire using the Garden slime launcher on the Genesis map or by using the Assassin enchantment on any map. Entering Spire in either manner does not grant the player participation in the event or stash their inventory.
Spire always generates in the same quadrant of each map – Temple on Abyss, Garden on Genesis, Winter on Seasons, Mountains on Elements, and Port on Castle.
