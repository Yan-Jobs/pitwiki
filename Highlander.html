Highlander
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Highlander is a megastreak in the Hypixel Pit. It is the fourth megastreak unlocked chronologically and the third purchasable megastreak. Unlocked in the Highlander Bundle at prestige VII (40 renown), it is a killstreak that triggers on 50 kills. Highlander can be used at level 60 and costs 30,000g to unlock.
The Highlander megastreak provides the player with three benefits:
  • Permanent Speed I.
  • Earn +110% gold from kills.
  • Deal +33% damage to players with a bounty.
On the Highlander megastreak, the player's maximum bounty increases to 10,000 gold. The player will also receive +0.3% damage from players with the Bounty Hunter perk for every kill above 50.
After the player dies, they will receive a copy of their bounty. If the amount gained by the killer was increased through the use of a Bounty Solvent, both players receive the bonus gold.
The Highlander Bundle also contains three killstreaks— Khanate, Rush, and Gold Nano-factory.

Most Pit players agree that the Highlander megastreak is the best megastreak for obtaining gold.
