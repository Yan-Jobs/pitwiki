Dark Pants
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Dark Pants are special types of mystic pants designed to counter Mysticism. Fresh dark pants can be crafted for 5 Chunks of Vile and 2 renown at night if the user has at least one tier of the Prestige VI renown upgrade Heresy. Every extra tier of Heresy the player has reduces the vile cost of Dark Pants by 1 Chunk of Vile. Dark pants are as strong as leather pants.
Dark pants function differently from fresh pants. Upgrading dark pants to tier I requires 10,000g and Heresy I, but unlike typical mystic pants, it does not require Mysticism IX. Tier I Dark Pants will always be enchanted with Somber and have 5–20 lives.

To upgrade to tier II, the player must have Mysticism IX, Heresy III, and 60,000g, at which point the pants will be enchanted with a random dark enchant.
Dark pants enchantments typically are very strong, but have a negative effect to the user to compensate.

Trivia

Dark Pants used to be as strong as chainmail pants. This was changed to leather in early 2020.
The time period the Nostalgia enchantment refers to is the time before the 0.3.5 update, when Protection I diamond armor could be found in Care Packages.
