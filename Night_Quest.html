Night Quest
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Night Quests are special tasks in the Hypixel Pit. They occur every 36 minutes at nightfall while the user has the Heresy renown upgrade.

When a night quest occurs, a chat message appears with the task to complete. The list of possible tasks is as follows:
  • Kill 30 players.
  • Kill 40 players.
  • Kill 50 players.
  • Kill 7 players without using mystic items.
  • Kill 12 players without using mystic items.
  • Get 16 kills without dying or returning to spawn.
  • Get 24 kills without dying or returning to spawn.
  • Get 20 kills without mystic items, dying or returning to spawn.

Upon completion, night quests add one Chunk of Vile to your inventory and grant 200 XP plus an additional 25 per prestige the player has. Trivia Night quests can be disabled in the Hersy menu. The Poet achievement is rewarded for completing your first night quest.
