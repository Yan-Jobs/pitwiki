Bounty
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Bounties are statuses added to players randomly after they reach a 6 kill streak. On kill, a player has a chance to receive a bounty, or if they already have one, for it to be increased (referred to as "bumping"). When the player is killed, their killer claims the amount of gold detailed in the bounty.

The higher a player's kill streak, the higher their initial bounty will be, and the higher their bounty bumps will be. Additionally, players will receive bounty bumps more frequently the quicker they kill players.

The maximum bounty a player can normally have is 5000g. This can be increased by to 10000g by the Highlander megastreak. Every 1000g in the player's bounty extends their combat timer by 5 seconds, up to an additional 25 seconds (5000g).

The amount of gold claimed from a bounty can be increased by 50% using a Bounty Solvent. Additionally, players using the Bounty Hunter perk earn assist shares of bounties.

With a Genesis tier V Faction perk, bounty bumps on yourself are increased by 50%.

Trivia

The Golden Handcuffs dark mystic shares bounties claimed with all players on the server.

The Everyone Gets a Bounty event allows players to bypass the bounty limit.
