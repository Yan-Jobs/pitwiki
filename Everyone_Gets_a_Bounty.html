Everyone Gets a Bounty
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Everyone Gets a Bounty, commonly shortened to "All Bounty", is a minor event in the Hypixel Pit.
The event is instantaneous. When it occurs, every non-bountied player receives a bounty of 100 gold. If a player already has a bounty, their bounty increases by 100. There is no limit bounties affected by the All Bounty eventâ€” due to this event, bounties have been seen as high as 99,000 gold.
